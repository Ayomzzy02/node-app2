About Us

JLogan Architecture Overview

A furniture design and construction firm founded in Amsterdam on the principle that design is a collaborative process that should yield inspiring spaces for the people that inhabit them. The process of design requires the duality of diving deep and thinking broadly. Furniture is inherently complicated and the design process is not always linear. As architects, we try to find elegant, sometimes bold solutions, to organize and simplify our designs. It’s especially challenging to communicate these ideas to clients who may have little background or experience in designing a building. The relationship with our clients, seeing the look of excitement in their eyes when they see the vision for the first time, is what motivates the firm.


Meet the CEO, Lucas Logan

After graduating from the Amsterdam School of the Arts, Founding Principal, Lucas Logan, began his career in 1997 working for Attika Architekten in North Holland, on large urban housing and mixed-use projects. After the experience of working for a large, international firm, Lucas worked for a sole practitioner, Robert Muller, in his hometown Flevoland. The juxtaposition of experiences between these two firms gave Lucas a broad perspective on architectural practice. Landmark Inc was founded in 2009 in His hometown Amsterdam and gained international Recognition in 2011.
